about my blog

I made it private

Hello there, My blog was public before, but as of April 10, 2023, I made it private. I intended it to become like E/N site, kind of a commonplace book.

I noticed that it is slowly turning out to be very personal. Which is what I intended for it to be in the first place. I created my blog so that every night, I can write down and reflect on what happened during my whole day. What I did. What I should not have done. What I can learn...

I proclaimed that it was not significant for the public to see or read and decided to keep it to myself.
